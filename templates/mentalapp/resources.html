Types of Support

Six Pathways to Support

Medical
Talk to Your Doctor

Your primary care physician can refer you to mental health specialists and rule out physical causes of symptoms.

Therapy
Therapy & Counseling

Licensed therapists offer evidence-based treatments like CBT, DBT, EMDR, and talk therapy tailored to your needs.

Crisis
Crisis Helplines

If you're in crisis, contact a helpline immediately. Most offer 24/7 free, confidential support by phone or chat.

Digital
Mental Health Apps

Apps like Headspace, Calm, and Woebot offer guided meditation and mood tracking as complements to professional care.

Community
Support Groups

Peer support groups connect you with others who understand your experience and reduce isolation.

Information
Online Resources

Trusted sources: WHO, NAMI, Mind.org, and Mental Health America — offering free education and service directories.

Digital Tools

Recommended Mental Health Apps

Headspace

Guided meditation and mindfulness exercises. Ideal for beginners building a daily practice.

Calm

Sleep stories, breathing exercises, and music for stress reduction and sleep improvement.

Woebot

An AI-powered CBT chatbot for daily check-ins and mood tracking. Available 24/7 for free.

Daylio

Micro-diary and mood tracker to identify patterns and emotional triggers over time.
