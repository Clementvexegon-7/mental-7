9 Key Conditions

Recognized Mental Health Conditions

Hover any card and use the "Check yourself" prompt for a quick personal reflection.

Mood
Depression

Persistent sadness, loss of interest, fatigue, and difficulty functioning. Affects 280 million people globally.

"Have you felt persistently sad or empty for more than 2 weeks?"

Anxiety
Anxiety Disorders

Excessive worry, fear, or panic that interferes with daily life. Includes GAD, social anxiety, panic disorder.

"Do you often feel anxious or worried in a way that's hard to control?"

Mood
Bipolar Disorder

Episodes of extreme mood swings — from manic highs to depressive lows — affecting sleep, energy, and behavior.

"Do you experience extreme highs (full of energy, little sleep needed) followed by very low periods?"

Trauma
PTSD

Post-traumatic stress disorder develops after exposure to a traumatic event, causing flashbacks and severe anxiety.

"Do you experience intrusive memories or flashbacks of a past traumatic event?"

Obsessive
OCD

Recurring, unwanted thoughts (obsessions) and repetitive behaviors (compulsions) that feel impossible to control.

"Do you feel compelled to repeat certain behaviors to relieve anxiety or distressing thoughts?"

Psychosis
Schizophrenia

A serious disorder affecting thinking, feelings, and behavior. May include hallucinations and delusions.

💚 Find professional support
Behavioral
Eating Disorders

Conditions like anorexia, bulimia, and binge-eating disorder involve distorted relationships with food and body image.

"Do you feel intense anxiety or guilt around eating, or have you restricted/changed eating significantly?"

Neurodevelopmental
ADHD

Affects focus, impulse control, and energy regulation. Common in children and often continues into adulthood.

"Do you struggle to focus, often act impulsively, or feel restless in ways that affect daily life?"

Personality
Borderline Personality

Intense emotional swings, unstable relationships, impulsive behavior, and an unstable sense of self.

"Do you experience intense and rapidly changing emotions, or fear of being abandoned by people close to you?"
